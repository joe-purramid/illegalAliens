Overview

2–6 aliens have crash-landed on Earth. To escape, each alien must harvest enough human organs to fool a government body scanner. Collect organs from various locations, steal from your fellow aliens, dodge the government Agent, and be the first to pass the BODY SCAN! at the Hospital to win.

Game Setup

Each player chooses a color (red, yellow, orange, green, blue, or purple) and receives:

· A colored Alien meeple and matching d6

· An organ-tracking chart

· 2 Organ cards and 2 Guile cards (3 Guile cards in 5–6 player games)

Randomly select a starting player. Each alien is placed at their designated crash landing space on the board. The starting player begins their turn and play proceeds clockwise.

The Board

Locations

Six locations form an oval around the outside of the board with 4 steps between each. Clockwise from top-left:

OLD FOLKS → GREASY SPORK → SPORTS! → BOOZ → NO-TEL → SCHÜL

The Hospital

The HOSPITAL sits at the center of the board, connected to each outer location by 5 steps. Players can travel around the oval or cut through the center.

Crash Landing Spaces (Starting Positions)

Each starting space is 2 steps to the left of a location:

L1: left of OLD FOLKS · L2: left of BOOZ · L3: left of SPORTS! · L4: left of SCHÜL · L5: left of GREASY SPORK · L6: left of NO-TEL

Turn Structure

Each player takes a complete turn before the next player goes. A turn has five phases:

1 READY!

Draw 2 cards from either the Organ deck, the Guile deck, or one from each. Player's choice.

2 SET!

Roll your d6 and apply any Guile card modifiers. The result is your Movement Value for this turn.

3 FLEE!

Move your alien meeple up to your Movement Value in steps. You can go left, right, or toward/from the Hospital.

· You must move at least 1 step.

· Locations count as 1 step of movement.

· You cannot cross the same step twice in the same turn.

· You cannot revisit a location without having visited a different location first. This rule persists across turns.

4 HARVEST!

You may take one or both of the following actions:

· Harvest from a location if you ended your movement on one.

· Harvest from a shipmate if you passed over another alien during movement (once per turn).

See the detailed sections below for harvesting rules.

5 HIDE!

Your turn is over. You may discard any unwanted cards from your hand, then draw back up to 4 cards (or 5 in a 5–6 player game) from either or both decks. If one deck is exhausted, draw from the other. If both are empty simultaneously, reshuffle both decks. If you already have 4 (or 5) or more cards, you do not draw. There is no hand limit. Play passes to the next player.

Harvesting Organs

The Six Organ Types

Intestine · Heart · Liver · Lung · Kidney · Stomach

Each organ can be tracked up to a maximum of 6 on your organ chart. You may never declare more organs than you have room to store.

How to Harvest at a Location

1. Declare which organ you want and how many you're attempting to collect.

2. Your target number = quantity declared + 1 (minimum target is always 2).

3. Roll your d6 and add up all modifiers:

· +1 per matching Organ card played (max cards played = number declared)

· +1 per matching organ on the ground at that location

· +/− any Guile card effects

· − location penalties (see below)

4. A natural 1 always fails, regardless of modifiers.

Outcome

· Result < target: Failure. You lose 1 of that organ type (if you have any). The lost organ falls to the ground at that location.

· Result ≥ target: Success! You gain the number of organs you declared. Any excess (result − target) falls to the ground at that location.

You're at OLD FOLKS and declare 4 livers (no penalty for livers here). Target = 5. You play 3 Liver organ cards. You roll a 4. Result = 4 + 3 = 7. Success! You keep 4 livers, and 7 − 5 = 2 excess livers fall to the ground.

Location Penalties

OLD FOLKS−1 Intestine
GREASY SPORK−1 Heart
SPORTS!−1 Liver
BOOZ−1 Lung
NO-TEL−1 Kidney
SCHÜL−1 Stomach

Hospital Rules

The Hospital has special rules for harvesting:

· Base −1 penalty to all organ rolls.

· You may harvest multiple organ types in a single action.

· Target number = total organs declared (across all types) + 1.

· An additional −1 per organ type being harvested (on top of the base −1).

· You may play matching Organ cards for each type, but no more cards of a type than the quantity declared for that type.

You declare 2 hearts and 2 livers at the Hospital. Target = 4 + 1 = 5. Modifiers: −1 (hospital) −1 (heart type) −1 (liver type) = −3 total. You play 2 Heart cards and 2 Liver cards (+4). You roll a 4. Result = 4 + 4 − 3 = 5. Success! You keep 2 hearts and 2 livers with no excess.

Harvesting from Shipmates

If you passed over another alien during FLEE!, you may attempt to steal one organ from them. This can only be attempted once per turn, regardless of how many aliens you passed. The target must have the organ you're trying to steal. All organ charts are public information.

How It Works

1. Declare the organ type you want to steal.

2. Play any Guile cards you wish. The target player may then respond with their own Guile cards. Players alternate playing cards until both pass or run out.

3. Both players make opposed d6 rolls, adding Guile modifiers. (Natural 1 is not an auto-fail on opposed rolls.)

4. Active player rolls strictly higher: Steal 1 organ of the declared type.

5. Target ties or rolls higher: No organ is stolen. The target immediately gets a free SET! roll and may move in any valid direction. The target cannot harvest from any aliens passed during this free move.

Organ Cards

The Organ deck contains 72 cards. Each card has two organs printed on it. Every possible pairing appears exactly twice.

Mixed Pairs (e.g., Liver/Intestine)

When played, choose which of the two organ types to use. It counts as +1 for that type.

Doubles (e.g., Liver/Liver)

Counts as +2 of that organ type when played.

Guile Cards

The Guile deck contains 72 cards. Each card has two effects: an Alien Guile effect and an Authority Guile effect. Choose which to use when played. Every Alien/Authority pairing appears exactly twice.

Guile cards may be played at any point during your turn before the HIDE! phase. Alien Guile effects can only be played on your own turn. Some Authority Guile effects can be played in response to other players' actions.

Alien Guile Effects

Computer Guidance! — +1 to your Harvest roll.
Tractor Beam! — +2 to your Harvest roll.
Data Hacking! — When landing on or passing over another alien during FLEE!, steal 2 cards at random from them. If passing over 2 aliens, you may steal 1 from each instead.
Hollow Ween! — Double all players' SET! rolls until your next READY! phase. Cancels an active Neighborhood Watch.
Immunity! — Remove all Germs! cards from one location. Can be played at any time during your turn.
Teleporter! — Before your SET! roll, move your alien to any crash landing space on the board.

Authority Guile Effects

Anonymous Tip! — −1 to an opponent's Harvest roll during a shipmate harvest against you.
Quadrupeds! — −2 to an opponent's Harvest roll during a shipmate harvest against you.
Sabotage! — When another alien lands on or passes over you during FLEE!, they discard 2 cards at random. Also cancels a Data Hacking! attempt (see below).
Neighborhood Watch! — Halve all players' SET! rolls (round down) until your next READY! phase. Cancels an active Hollow Ween.
Germs! — Place this card beside a location. −1 to all Harvest rolls there. Stackable. Cannot be placed at the Hospital.
Men in Blarknap! — Move the Agent one location in any direction (see Agent rules).

Data Hacking! VS Sabotage!

These cards cancel each other one-for-one. If Data Hacking is played and the target responds with Sabotage, both are cancelled — no cards are stolen and no cards are discarded. Players may continue to play these cards; any unmatched card takes full effect. If Data Hacking targets two aliens, a single Sabotage cancels the entire attempt.

The Agent

The Agent is a government operative hunting the aliens. The Agent meeple starts at the Hospital and uses a black die.

Moving the Agent

When a Men in Blarknap! card is played, the player moves the Agent to an adjacent location (one step along the ring or to/from the Hospital). On the very first move of the game, the Agent may be sent to any of the 6 outer locations. The Agent stays put until the next Men in Blarknap card is played.

Agent Encounters

When the Agent moves, any aliens it passes over or that are at the Agent's destination must make opposed d6 rolls against the Agent. No modifiers apply to these rolls. Instead of rolling, a player may play a Teleporter! card to escape the encounter.

· Player rolls higher: The player escapes and gets a free SET! roll to move.

· Player ties or rolls lower: The player is sent to the Hospital and loses 1 organ of the type they have the fewest of (player's choice if tied). The lost organ is removed from the game, not dropped on the ground.

Wanted!

If an alien is sent to the Hospital by the Agent and does not have at least one of every organ type, they receive a Wanted! token instead of losing an organ. If the same alien is sent to the Hospital by the Agent a second time and still does not have one of every organ type, that alien is eliminated from the game.

Passive Effect

Any location where the Agent is present has an additional −1 penalty to all organ harvest rolls. Simply passing the Agent during normal movement does not trigger a contest — only Men in Blarknap does.

Winning the Game

BODY SCAN!

Collect a number of each organ type equal to the number of players in the game (e.g., 3 of each in a 3-player game). Then reach the HOSPITAL and take the BODY SCAN! action in place of your HARVEST! phase.

No dice roll required. The first player to take a successful BODY SCAN! wins the game.

BODY SCAN! replaces HARVEST! — you cannot harvest at the Hospital and body scan on the same visit.
